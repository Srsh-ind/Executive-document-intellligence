Quarterly Business Performance Review – Q2 FY2025
Executive Summary
The company delivered strong revenue growth during Q2 FY2025, driven primarily by Enterprise customers and expansion into international markets. Revenue increased by 18.4% compared to Q1, while EBITDA margins improved due to better operational efficiency.
However, customer acquisition costs increased and supply chain delays affected delivery timelines. Leadership should prioritize operational improvements and accelerate digital initiatives to sustain growth.
